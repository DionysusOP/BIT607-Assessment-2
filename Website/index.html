Haukai Restaurant

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. The owners work in the business full-time. They have only two employees at present, but in the future, they hope to take on additional part-time staff. The restaurant is only open in the evenings at the moment, from Wednesday to Sunday inclusive

The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. See the Menu for the current restaurant dishes available.
